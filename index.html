Under the influence of his parents, Yuanhao Li began to learn photography from primary school, and he began to learn photography knowledge systematically in middle school.

Media Practice

As a student major in the School of Journalism and Communication, Yuanhao Li has accumulated a lot of media practice experience in and out of the university during his two years in university. 

In addition to photography, which he is proud of, his practice in the field of interview and press release writing is also worthy of attention.

 

Sept. 2019

New Tide

In the college media New Tide , Yuanhao Li participated in the shooting and writing of more than ten articles, and independently completed some of them. In the new school year, he will become the head of the media's photography department.

The Observation of Epidemic

When coVID-19 hit, Li joined the epidemic prevention and observation program, and independently completed an in-depth report on vegetable supply during the epidemic in Xi 'an. The report was eventually forwarded by authoritative media and included in campus Media Epidemic Prevention Observation, a book produced by the School of Journalism and Communication.
